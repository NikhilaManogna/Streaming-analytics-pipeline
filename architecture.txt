Component Notes
---------------

1. Producer
   Generates click, order, and payment events with a small duplicate rate and a small malformed-event rate. Retries are enabled to reduce transient publish failures.

2. Kafka
   Buffers incoming events on the `events` topic. Topic creation is explicit and configured with three partitions.

3. Spark Streaming
   Consumes Kafka records, parses JSON, filters invalid payloads, drops duplicates using `event_id`, applies a two-minute watermark, computes one-minute aggregates, and writes malformed records to a dead-letter table.

4. PostgreSQL
   Stores both serving metrics and invalid-event records. Aggregate rows are upserted by minute window so open windows stay current.

5. Streamlit
   Reads the serving tables and renders revenue, active users, per-minute event volume, anomaly signals, and dead-letter summaries.
